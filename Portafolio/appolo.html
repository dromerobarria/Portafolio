Oktoberfest Chile 2014

Share contests and promotions.

Oktoberfest Chile 2014 is an App to share contests and promotions , and it is being tested as part of Oktoberfest 2014 , the massive event that brings together prominent craft breweries in Malloco , Chile.

How it works.

The app “Oktoberfest 2014 Chile” works by sending beats or notifications to mobile devices with a sound that alert the user when an opportunity is available so you can take it. For this, they have a web platform that manages in which you can define certain areas fences or action , where the customers or where activity occurs .
